# Privacy Policy

**Tadabbur**
Last updated: April 7, 2026

## Overview

Tadabbur ("the App") is a daily Quran contemplation app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data (if you sign in)
- Name, email address, and profile photo from Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In
- This data is used solely to identify your account and enable cloud sync

### Usage Data
- Your current verse position and streak count
- Journal reflections you write (stored locally and in cloud if signed in)
- App preferences (language, reciter, font size)
- Feedback you submit through the in-app feedback form

### Analytics Data
- Anonymous app usage events (ayah completed, reflection added)
- Device type, operating system, and country
- Crash reports for improving app stability
- We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for this purpose

## How We Use Your Data

- **To provide the service**: storing your progress, syncing across devices, delivering daily ayat
- **To improve the app**: understanding usage patterns, fixing crashes, reading feedback
- **We never sell your data**
- **We never share your personal data with third parties** except as required by Firebase (Google) for the services listed above

## Data Storage

- **Local data**: stored on your device using SharedPreferences and encrypted secure storage
- **Cloud data**: stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) if you sign in
- **Auth tokens**: encrypted using platform-native secure storage (Android EncryptedSharedPreferences, iOS Keychain)

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase** (authentication, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting) — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Quran Foundation API** (Quran text, translations, tafsir, audio) — [quran.com](https://quran.com)
- **Islamic Network CDN** (audio recitation files)
- **Google Sign-In** and **Apple Sign-In** for authentication

## Your Rights

- **Delete your data**: uninstalling the app removes all local data. To delete cloud data, contact us at thetadabburapp@gmail.com
- **Guest mode**: use the app without signing in — no personal data is collected beyond anonymous analytics
- **Opt out of analytics**: disable analytics in your device settings

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or our website.
